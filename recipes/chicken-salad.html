Chicken Salad

Description

What is the classification of chicken salad? The term bound is most often used for traditional mixtures of cooked protein, starch and vegetables items with mayonnaise like chicken salad, tuna salad, egg salad and potato salad

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery.
